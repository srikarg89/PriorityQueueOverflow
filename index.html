About TakeOffTime

TakeOffTime is a JavaScript rocket launch simulator that has an interactive GUI, incorporates 3D models, and can be used for model rockets.

Main Attributes

Contains a GUI that was made in three.js

Calculates apogee, max velocity, average velocity, acceleration, thrust, drag, and much more

Graphics that were made in p5.js

About Us:

We are a team of programmers from Thomas Jefferson High School of Science and Technology who love to code in many languages. We all have a good interest in space science and rocketry, which along with our skill sets, helped us get inspired to make this project.
